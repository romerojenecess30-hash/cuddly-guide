## How to Measure

### For Shirts
- **Chest/Bust**: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape measure horizontal
- **Length**: Measure from the highest point of the shoulder to the desired length

### For Pants
- **Waist**: Measure around your natural waistline (belly button level)
- **Hip**: Measure around the fullest part of your hips
- **Inseam**: Measure from the crotch seam to the bottom of the ankle

### For Shoes
- **Foot Length**: Stand on a piece of paper, mark your heel and longest toe, then measure the distance
- Measure both feet and use the larger measurement
- Measure at the end of the day when feet are slightly swollen

## Fit Guide

### Shirts
- **Slim Fit**: Fitted through chest and waist
- **Regular Fit**: Standard comfortable fit with room to move
- **Relaxed Fit**: Loose, comfortable fit

### Pants
- **Skinny**: Very fitted throughout the leg
- **Slim**: Fitted through thigh and leg
- **Regular**: Standard straight-leg fit
- **Relaxed**: Loose, comfortable fit

---

*Note: Measurements may vary slightly by brand and style. When in doubt, size up for comfort.*
